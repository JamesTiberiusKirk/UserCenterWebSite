Stationary Computer > Apple (iMac, MacBook) > Connect to WiFi


1. Click in the wifi icon          in the menu bar


     1.a If Wi-Fi is off        , click ,then selectTurn Wi-Fi On.*


2. Choose a network


3.a If you connect to a public Wi-Fi network, a window might appear with terms and conditions you're asked to agree to before you can connect

3.b Secure Wi-Fi networks are password-protected and have


a lock icon        by their names.

When connecting to them you will be asked for a password. If it is your home wifi the password is typically on the back of the router box(the box giving you internet). At the user centre please ask a helper for additional assistance.
