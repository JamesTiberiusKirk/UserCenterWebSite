Smartphone > Windows  (Nokia Lumia) > Simple tutorials

 Update your apps


1. Tap on this icon on your phone       (The store icon)

2. Then tap this icon       >Updates and Downloads.


     *Quick tip usually your phone settings will be on to update automatically.

3. Tap Check for updates > Update all.


If you want to switch off automatic updates.

1. Tap store        >Go to settings > toggle  the button to turn Update of apps off


 Personalize action centre.

1. Swipe down from the top screen.

2.Tap All settings     >System   > Notifications and actions.

To change a shortcut, tap the shortcut and the app you would

like to replace with.

Also you can customise which notifications you would like to

see on the action centre.
