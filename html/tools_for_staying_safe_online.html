Here we have compiled a set of tools you can use to aid your efferts to protect yourself online


Password Manager

Having passwords that are hard to guess is important especially on the internet where people might attempt to cunningly guess them. It is also important to keep different passwords for every different website you log into. However, it can be hard for anyone to remember a lot of different passwords so password managers were made to do this for you.


As the name suggest password managers, manage your passwords. They not only manage your passwords but also suggest strong passwords that are hard to break. You'll be glad to know that you don't have to remember these randomly generated passwords because the managers do it for you.


Well, so how do you access your passwords? Password managers work on the basis that you have one master password which you type in to access all your other passwords. A good password manager remembers for which website to use which passwords.


Why have many different passwords? If a hacker gets a hold of one of you passwords for one account on a website (e.g. facebook), then all your other accounts on other websites are still safe because you have different password for them. It would also be really hard for a hacker to steal your master password.
