Stationary PC > Windows > Send Email    click here for common email terms


1. Locate your task bar search, usually it will be bottom left side of the screen.








2. Click on it and a screen should pop up, like this:























3. If you start typing the word "chrome", the application should pop up in within the first few letters.






















4. Click on the application.

5. When the application is loaded, locate the link bar at the top of the screen and type in "gmail.com".




















6. Here you need to know your log in and password. Use the Next button to type it in one by one.





















7. Once logged in you should see a screen similar to this. Use the Compose button at the top left of the screen to compose an email.


















8. After clicking the Compose button from previous step, you should get a boxpop up in the bottom right corner of your screen that looks like this.






















9. Now you need to make sure that you have the address of the recipient whom you want to sent a letter to.





















10. Enter in a subject line (this could be anything) and enter some text into the body of the email.





















11. Click send to send the email.






















12. You have now successfully sent an email
