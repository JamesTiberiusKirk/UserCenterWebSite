Internet Safety

Smartphone > Apple (iPhone) > Connect to WiFi


1. Find the settings icon on the home screen and tap on it
















2. Once in settings tap on "Wi-Fi"
















3. Once in the WiFi screen you should see a list of nearby WiFi networks, if you don't see wifi networks make sure to tap on the box in the top right of the screen so it appears green.
















4. Now tap on the network you want to connect to. If connecting to your home network check the back of the WiFi router/modem, you should be able to find the name of your home network there, along with the password.


If at the user centre ask a helper for WiFi access details.


Take precaution when accessing public networks to make sure they are safe. Click here to learn how to identify a safe public network.


5. Now you will be prompted to enter the password to the network you are attempting to connect to. Enter the password and in then at the top right of your screen click "join".
