User Centre Talk - e-reader services – Friday 14th December 10:50am

User Centre Talk - Disability Services – Thursday 22nd November 10.50am

2019 will mark the 14th year since the User centre opened back in 2005. When architects were designing the building one of the requirements was to have a space for older people and technology to converge in the form of research and a members club. A small celebration will be made at the User centre with some cake.

Alistair Wilson is having a short introductory talk about e-reader services available in local libraries in the Dundee area. Anyone interested in expending their knowledge of technology and want to experience a new way of reading books should consider attending in the User Centre!

User Centre Helpers and Students will be making introductions to the new page and its usages such as the brand new dedicated tutorial section. Come along to learn about the newest tool in helping everyone in the user centre find their way around today’s technology!

This month's User Centre talk will be delivered by Shirley Hill, Head of Disability Services at the University of Dundee. Shirley will be talking about the role of Disability Services at the University of Dundee, such as the services they provide staff and students and what everyone can do to help or receive help!
